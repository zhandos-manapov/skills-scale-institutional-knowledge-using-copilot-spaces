# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA/Test Lead

### Role Summary
QA/Test Leads own the quality strategy, test planning, and acceptance validation. They ensure that work meets acceptance criteria and quality standards before release.

### Responsibilities
- Define and maintain test plans and QA approach for the project
- Create and execute acceptance test cases
- Identify and triage defects
- Collaborate with Developers on testability and test automation
- Validate release readiness before deployment
- Track quality metrics (test coverage, defect escape rate)

### Goals
- Ensure quality and user confidence in delivered features
- Reduce defect escape rate through comprehensive testing
- Enable fast feedback loops and continuous quality improvement

### Interaction with other roles
- **Developers:** Partner on unit testing, test automation, and testable design
- **Product Manager:** Clarify acceptance criteria and prioritize edge cases
- **Project Manager:** Provide status on test completion and quality gate decisions

### Typical Communication
- Sprint planning and QA review meetings
- Defect reports and quality status updates
- Test strategy and acceptance criteria alignment sessions

---

## Technical Lead

### Role Summary
Technical Leads guide architecture decisions, technical standards, and code quality. They ensure solutions are scalable, maintainable, and aligned with the technology roadmap.

### Responsibilities
- Define technical architecture and design patterns
- Establish code quality and standards (linting, testing, documentation)
- Conduct technical design reviews
- Identify technical risks and propose mitigations
- Mentor Developers on best practices
- Support technical capacity planning and effort estimation

### Goals
- Build scalable, maintainable systems
- Reduce technical debt and rework
- Enable team velocity through good design and tooling

### Interaction with other roles
- **Developers:** Review designs, set standards, mentor on implementation
- **Project Manager:** Advise on technical feasibility and schedule risks
- **Product Manager:** Educate on technical trade-offs and constraints

### Typical Communication
- Technical design review meetings
- Architecture decision records (ADRs)
- Code review guidance and technical mentoring sessions

---

## Stakeholder/Sponsor

### Role Summary
Stakeholders and Sponsors provide business context, approve scope, and remove organizational blockers. They ensure the project aligns with business priorities and has executive visibility.

### Responsibilities
- Provide business requirements and context
- Approve project scope, timeline, and budget
- Remove organizational and resource blockers
- Make executive-level decisions on trade-offs
- Communicate project status to leadership
- Validate business outcomes and ROI

### Goals
- Ensure projects deliver measurable business value
- Maintain strategic alignment and priority
- Remove barriers to delivery

### Interaction with other roles
- **Project Manager:** Receive status updates and escalations; make decisions on scope/priority
- **Product Manager:** Approve go/no-go decisions and validate success metrics
- **Developers:** Understand business rationale and constraints

### Typical Communication
- Monthly or milestone-based status reviews
- Executive steering committee meetings
- Decision-making on scope and trade-offs

---

## Scrum Master / Delivery Facilitator

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters and Delivery Facilitators remove impediments and ensure process adherence. They enable the team to work efficiently by removing blockers and optimizing workflows.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate daily standups, planning, and retrospectives
- Identify and escalate team impediments
- Monitor sprint velocity and team health
- Coach the team on Agile practices
- Protect the team from scope creep and distractions
- Track process metrics and recommend improvements

### Goals
- Maximize team productivity and flow
- Build a high-performing, self-organizing team
- Continuous process improvement

### Interaction with other roles
- **Project Manager:** Escalate organizational blockers and provide team status
- **Developers:** Remove technical blockers and process friction
- **Product Manager:** Facilitate backlog refinement and prioritization discussions

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint ceremonies
- One-on-one coaching and obstacle resolution
- Process improvement retrospectives

---

## Security Officer

### Role Summary
Security Officers embed security requirements and conduct risk assessments throughout the project lifecycle. They ensure features are secure and compliant.

### Responsibilities
- Review requirements and designs for security implications
- Define security acceptance criteria and testing
- Conduct threat modeling for high-risk features
- Coordinate security scanning and code reviews
- Escalate and track security incidents
- Ensure compliance with organizational policies

### Goals
- Prevent security vulnerabilities in production
- Reduce incident response time and business impact
- Build customer trust through secure, compliant products

### Interaction with other roles
- **Developers:** Provide secure coding guidance and review implementations
- **QA/Test Lead:** Define security test cases and validation approach
- **Project Manager:** Escalate security risks and manage remediation timelines

### Typical Communication
- Security review meetings and threat modeling sessions
- Security incident and vulnerability reports
- Compliance and audit coordination

---

## UX/Design Lead

### Role Summary
UX/Design Leads define user experience, design standards, and usability acceptance. They ensure features are intuitive, accessible, and delightful for end users.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and validate user needs
- Define design standards and component libraries
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Establish accessibility and usability criteria
- Conduct design reviews and usability testing
- Ensure consistency across the product

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible, and delightful user experiences
- Reduce user onboarding time and support burden
- Increase user satisfaction and retention

### Interaction with other roles
- **Product Manager:** Validate user needs and prioritize features
- **Developers:** Review implementations for design fidelity and accessibility
- **QA/Test Lead:** Define usability acceptance criteria and testing approach

### Typical Communication
- User research sessions and design reviews
- Design specifications and accessibility audits
- Usability testing and feedback sessions
